About us

Where geology meets thoughtful design.

Quality Granite and Marble was founded on a simple belief: the world's finest natural stone deserves craftspeople who know it inside out. Every slab in our yard is hand-selected from quarries across Italy, Brazil, Turkey, and India.

From kitchen islands to hotel lobbies, we bring geological beauty into the places where life happens — with precision cutting, meticulous finishing, and installation built to endure.

Our materials

Three stones. Infinite possibilities.

Each material carries its own character. We help you choose the right one for how you live and how the space performs.

01

Marble

Calacatta, Statuario, Nero Marquina — metamorphic elegance from Mediterranean quarries. Timeless in any application.

02

Granite

Igneous durability with crystalline depth. Ideal for high-traffic surfaces — over 40 origins in stock.

03

Travertine

Warm limestone formed over millennia. Perfect for floors, facades, and outdoor spaces with Mediterranean character.

Stone for every room and scale.

How we work

Precision at every stage.

From first visit to final polish, our process is transparent and focused entirely on the result you have in mind.

01

Consultation

We visit your site, understand your vision, and walk you through our inventory — in person or virtually.

02

Slab selection

You choose your exact slab. No surprises — what you approve is precisely what we install.

03

Fabrication

CNC waterjet cutting paired with hand-finishing. Edge profiles, inlays, and custom cuts — all in-house.

04

Installation

Our teams install with care, seal the stone, and leave your space clean and ready to enjoy for decades.
