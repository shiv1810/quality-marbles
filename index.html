About

Where geology
meets design.

Pietra Stone Co. was founded on a single conviction: that the world's finest natural stone deserves to be handled by craftspeople who understand it deeply. Every slab in our inventory has been hand-selected from quarries in Italy, Brazil, Turkey, and India.

From kitchen countertops to hotel lobbies, we bring geological beauty into the places where life happens — with precision cutting, meticulous finishing, and installation that treats every project as a permanent statement.

Our Materials

Each stone tells
its own story.

01

Marble

Calacatta, Statuario, Nero Marquina — classic metamorphic elegance from the Italian Alps and Mediterranean quarries. Timeless in any application.

02

Granite

Igneous durability with natural crystalline beauty. Superior hardness for high-use surfaces. Available in over 40 origins, from Brazilian Blue to Kashmir White.

03

Travertine

The warmth of ancient limestone, formed over millennia. Ideal for flooring, facades, and outdoor spaces seeking a natural Mediterranean character.

How We Work

Precision at
every stage.

From first consultation to final polish, our process is designed to be thorough, transparent, and entirely focused on the result you have in mind.

01

Consultation

We visit the site, understand your vision, and walk you through our full inventory — in person or virtually.

02

Slab Selection

You view and choose your exact slab. No surprises — what you approve is precisely what we install.

03

Fabrication

CNC waterjet cutting combined with hand-finishing by our master craftspeople. Edge profiles, inlays, and custom cuts are all handled in-house.

04

Installation

Our installation teams work with precision and care. We leave the site clean, the stone sealed, and the work ready to last a lifetime.
