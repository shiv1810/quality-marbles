About us

Craftsmanship you can see in every edge

Quality Granite and Marble has served homeowners, builders, and designers since 1987. Every slab in our yard is selected for quality and character, then cut and finished in-house to your exact specifications.

Whether you need a kitchen countertop, bathroom vanity, commercial lobby, or exterior cladding, our team guides you from slab selection through installation.

  • Bathroom vanities
  • Flooring & cladding
  • Commercial interiors

Our process

From consultation to installation

  1. 1

    Consultation

    We discuss your project, take measurements, and review options in our showroom or at your site.

  2. 2

    Slab selection

    You choose the exact slab from our inventory — what you approve is what we fabricate.

  3. 3

    Fabrication

    Precision cutting, edge profiling, and finishing carried out in our workshop.

  4. 4

    Installation

    Professional fitting, sealing, and a clean handover — ready for daily use.
